## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### High-Risk Items

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|-----------|
| AI/ML complexity | Medium | High | Start with simpler models, expert consulting |
| Multi-tenancy overhead | Medium | Medium | Early prototyping, load testing |
| Integration delays | Medium | Medium | Modular architecture, early partner engagement |
| Performance degradation | Low | High | Continuous benchmarking, optimization |
| Security vulnerabilities | Low | High | Security audits, penetration testing |

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Technical Complexity**
   - Phased rollout approach
   - Expert consultation for AI/ML
   - Early prototyping and validation

2. **Timeline Pressure**
   - Clear prioritization (Tier 1 first)
   - Parallel track development
   - Aggressive testing from day 1

3. **Integration Challenges**
   - Partner early engagement
   - Clear API contracts
   - Sandbox environments

4. **Resource Constraints**
   - Flexible hiring/contracting
   - Training investments
   - Knowledge sharing
